Odin Family Recipes


Kimchi


  • 2 napa cabbage(Brined, rinsed, and cut into 1 inch pieces)
  • 1 cup kosher salt
  • 1 bulb garlic (peeled)
  • 1 2" piece ginger
  • 1.5 - 2 cups gochugaru
  • 1/4 cup fish sauce
  • 1 TBSP soy sauce
  • 2 medium carrots(peeled and grated)
  • 1 bunch green onions(cut into 2 inch lengths)
  • 1 TBSP shrimp paste

    • Porridge

    • 2 TBSP rice flour
    • 2 TBSP brown sugar
    • 2 cups water

Dry Brine


  1. Rub salt thoroughly on all aspects of quartered napa cabbage
  2. Place in a large bowl, flipping every 2 hours for 6 to 8 hours. When a leaf can be bent back without breaking.

Porridge


  1. Whisk rice flour and water together, bring to a boil.
  2. Remove from heat and add brown sugar.
  3. Allow to cool before adding to kimchi base.
  1. Place garlic, ginger, fish sauce, soy sauce, shrimp paste, gochugaru in a food processor.
  2. Add cooled porridge to bowl of food processor blitz until smooth, place in large bowl.
  3. Add carrots, green onions to kimchi base, mix well.
  4. Add napa cabbage to bowl, toss until cabbage is well coated.
  5. Put kimchi in large clean jars, compacting it to remove air.
  6. Let sit at room temperature for 2-3 days, until preferred taste.
